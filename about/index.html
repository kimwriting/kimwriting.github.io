# About Me

Hi there! I’m Kim Johnstone, a bit of a word geek with a background in health. My focus has been on holistic family wellness with a focus on breastfeeding support as an International Board Certified Lactation Consultant.

I share my home in Alberta, Canada with my husband, our three kiddos, and just enough pets to keep me flush with dog hair.

I’ve been working in the health field since 2001. I have presented at conferences, worked in private practice, and have had my work published online and in print.
