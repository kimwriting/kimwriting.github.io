# About Me

Kim Johnstone is a writer living in Alberta, Canada. Her background in the healthcare industry includes certification as an Equine Sports Therapist, as a Holistic Health Practitioner, and as an International Board Certified Lactation Consultant.

She has a strong interest in supporting family health and wellness. She teaches classes, works with clients one-on-one, has had her work published online and in print, and is available for public speaking engagements.
